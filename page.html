Safety Information
If pregnant or lactating, consult your physician before taking this product. NAC or vitamin C may cause gastrointestinal discomfort in sensitive individuals. Vitamin C should not be taken by individuals with glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase deficiency, iron overload, a history of kidney stones, or kidney failure.Vitamin C may react with mixed amphetamines (a specific amphetamine combination used to treat narcolepsy and attention deficit disorder) and blood thinning medications.

5.0 out of 5 starsNo more early signs of macular degeneration!
ByBrenda L. Phillipson August 15, 2016
Package Type: Standard Packaging
Approximately 6-7 years ago, my eye exam revealed early signs of macular degeneration. Fortunately, my ophthalmologist was progressive and told me of some research that showed that certain vitamins seemed to help. I did some research of my own, and started out geting some local drug store supplements, lutein, bilberry, and grape seed extract. It was a lot of pills to take! There were no complete eye supplements at the time. Even now, I have looked at many of the ones that are now available at the drugstore, none of them have all of these supplements, some do not have ANY of them! About 4-5 years ago, a friend turned me on to Pure Encapulations, and was thrilled to see an actual "Macular Support" supplement! It not only contained all three ingredients I had determined to be important, but there were other vitamins making it a more complete supplement. I have been using it ever since, and now my eye exam reveals healthy eyes, with NO evidence of macular degeneration at all! I highly recommend this formula for anyone concerned about their eye health.
I recently lost my wholesale connection for the purchase of this supplement, and have not been taking anything for several months. My eyes are still healthy so far, but my vision seems to be deteriorating. For example, right now I have to keep one eye closed to be able to read and write this review. When I try to open both eyes, the text looks burry, doulbled, ghost-like, etc.! I cannot focus my eyes to see one image. So, I am here at Amazon to get back on this supplement, pronto!
